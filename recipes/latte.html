A Latte In All Its Glory!

Ingredients:

  • Espresso Beans
  • Milk
  • Coffee Machine
  • Glass
  • Steaming Pitcher

Steps:

  1. Pour the milk into the steaming pitcher
  2. Put the espresso beans into the coffee machine and pull your espresso shot into the glass
  3. While the espresso shot is pulling steam your milk using the coffee machine
  4. Make sure to steam the milk in a way that it creates minimal foam
  5. Pour the steamed milk over the espresso shot, taking care to ensure that a lorge portion of the milk goes in and a small amount of foam
  6. Enjoy your latte!
