A Coffee In All Its Beauty

Ingredients:

  • Espresso Beans
  • Coffee Machine
  • Glass
  • Hot Water

Steps:

  1. Put the espresso beans into the coffee machine and put hot water into the coffee machine
  2. Set the coffee machine to steep the coffee beans in hot liquid (Drip Setting)
  3. Place your glass over the exit nozzle of the machine to catch the coffee
  4. Enjoy your coffee!
