An Espresso In All Its Glory

Ingredients:

  • Espresso Beans
  • Coffee Machine
  • Glass
  • Hot Water

Steps:

  1. Put the espresso beans into the coffee machine and put hot water into the coffee machine
  2. Pull a espresso shot using the espresso setting on your coffee machine
  3. Place your glass over the exit nozzle of the machine to catch the espresso
  4. Enjoy your espresso!
